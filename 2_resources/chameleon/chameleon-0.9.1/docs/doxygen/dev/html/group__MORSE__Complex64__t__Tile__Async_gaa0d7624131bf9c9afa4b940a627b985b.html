MORSE_zlauum_Tile_Async - Computes the product U * U' or L' * L, where the triangular factor U or L is stored in the upper or lower triangular part of the array A. Non-blocking equivalent of MORSE_zlauum_Tile(). May return before the computation is finished. Allows for pipelining of operations at runtime.

[in]sequenceIdentifies the sequence of function calls that this call belongs to (for completion checks and exception handling purposes).
